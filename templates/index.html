Instructions

Adding The Store Card
  1. Click "Your Payments"
  2. Click "Settings"
  3. Under "Payment Preference", Click "Set a preference", then "change"
  4. Select "Add a Credit or Debit"
  5. Enter your account name with the store card number
  6. Click "Add your card"
  7. Enter your billing address
Recommended Increments

$50 → $100 → $125 → $150 → $200... etc

Stay at one increment for a few orders and slowly build up.

Important Notes
  • Account must be 6+ months old with 1+ order
  • Browse site before/after linking card
  • Wait 5+ days after linking before ordering
  • Wait for pending orders to deliver + 1-2 days
  • Single items have higher success rate
  • Order items as gifts
