Card Addition Steps:
  1. Navigate to "Your Payments"
  2. Access "Settings"
  3. Find "Payment Preference" and select "Set a preference"
  4. Choose "Add a Credit or Debit"
  5. Enter account name and generated card number
  6. Complete with billing information
Recommended Usage Pattern:

Progressive increments: $50 → $100 → $125 → $150 → $200

Maintain consistent increment levels for multiple transactions

Best Practices:
  • Account age: 6+ months with purchase history
  • Waiting period: 5+ days post-linking
  • Order completion: Wait for delivery + 48 hours
  • Preference: Single item transactions
  • Gift option: Enable for better success rate
